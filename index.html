about me

I'm Hannah and I've always got my nose buried in a book! My deep passion for books sparked my interest in sharing my book reviews on social media and I have been blessed by the wonderful community and found new found friends. Books have the power to transform lives and with the portrayals of different worlds , each story has the power to leave a mark on our hearts and minds. From heart-warming contemporary fiction to mind -bending dystopian, I love in to indulge in a wide array of genres. I believe there's beauty in diversity and I am always on the lookout for narratives that challenge perspectives and broaden horizons. That being said, I am a born again Christian and do cross the line on books that I feel that stray away from my faith or I have decided that they have questionable content in them. One thing I enjoy about the bookish community is that it consists of a wide range of readers from those who enjoy sweet clean romance to terrifying dark thrillers. In my reviews, I strive to provide insightful and honest thoughts hoping to guide you into next favorite read!
